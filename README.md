# Digital-literacy-project #
 ## Project Overview ##

This repository contains my Digital Literacy Project, which focuses on understanding responsible digital behavior, safe internet practices, and the effective use of digital tools. The project includes infographics, case studies, email etiquette exercises, and cybersecurity awareness materials.

The goal of this project is to help students develop essential digital literacy skills, including safe online communication, responsible social media use, and awareness of cybercrime and online fraud.

## Module Summaries ##
### Module 1 – Understanding Digital Literacy ###

This module explains the concept of digital literacy and why it is important for students in the modern digital world. It covers topics such as finding reliable information online, using digital platforms responsibly, and maintaining a professional online presence.

### Module 2 – Digital Tools for Students ###

This section introduces useful digital tools that help students improve productivity and collaboration. Examples include Google Docs and Google Drive for document creation and storage, Zoom or Google Meet for online communication, and Canva for presentations and infographics.

### Module 3 – Safe Internet Practices ###

This module focuses on cybersecurity awareness and responsible internet usage. It discusses practices such as using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links or downloads, protecting personal information, and verifying information before sharing it online.

### Module 4 – Email Etiquette ###

This section demonstrates how to write professional emails in academic and professional environments. It includes examples such as requesting assignment extensions and applying for internship opportunities.

### Module 5 – Cybercrime Awareness ###

This module explains common cybercrimes such as phishing, online payment fraud, and identity theft. It also includes a case study and a prevention checklist with tips to help students stay safe online.

## Purpose of the Project ##

The purpose of this project is to help students become responsible digital citizens by learning safe online practices, improving digital communication skills, and understanding cyber threats.
