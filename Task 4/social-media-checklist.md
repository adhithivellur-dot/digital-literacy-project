# DO'S #
1.Use strong privacy settings to control who can see your posts and personal information.
2.Think before posting to ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
3.Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
4.Maintain a professional online presence that reflects positively on you.
5.Respect others online by communicating politely and avoiding offensive comments.

# DON'TS #
1.Don’t share personal information such as phone numbers, addresses, or passwords publicly.
2.Don’t post inappropriate or harmful content that could damage your reputation.
3.Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online harassment.
4.Don’t click on suspicious links or downloads from unknown sources.
5.Don’t spend excessive time on social media that affects studies or productivity.
